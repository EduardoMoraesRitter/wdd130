São Paulo, Brazil

São Paulo is my favorite city. It is the largest city in Brazil and one of the most populous in the world, a place where dozens of cultures meet in the same streets. Its endless skyline, its food, and its restless energy make it feel alive at every hour of the day.

From the historic downtown to neighborhoods like Avenida Paulista, there is always something to discover. It is a city of opportunity, creativity, and people from every corner of the planet.
