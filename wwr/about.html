Rio Verde Rafting

At Rio Verde Rafting our mission is to deliver unforgettable white water adventures while keeping every guest safe. We believe the river brings people together, builds courage, and creates memories that last a lifetime.

History

Rio Verde Rafting was founded in 1998 by a small group of river guides who shared a passion for adventure. What began with a single raft and a dream has grown into one of the most trusted rafting outfitters in the region, guiding thousands of explorers down the rapids each season.
